The routing calculator

One number, the Lerner Index, splits your traffic into two markets. Frontier reasoning sits near 1: a handful of sellers, high pricing power. Classify, extract, summarize sits near 0: dozens of good-enough models, and the seller loses pricing power the moment you can shop. Route each request to its layer and see what happens to the bill.

Routing math: frontier-for-everything = volume × frontier price. Routed = volume × (frontier share × frontier price + commodity share × commodity price). Feature math: cost per request = (fresh input ÷ 1M × price) + (cached ÷ 1M × cached price) + (output ÷ 1M × price) + tool cost, all × (1 + retry rate). Monthly bill = cost per request × requests per user × users.
