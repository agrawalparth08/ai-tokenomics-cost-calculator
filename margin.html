Above this many outcomes a month, a user loses you money on a $50 flat plan. Everyone past the line is subsidized by everyone below it.

Margin = flat price − (outcomes × cost per outcome). Break-even = price ÷ cost per outcome. This is the exact trap from the video “Your Best Customers Are Quietly Your Biggest AI Loss.” Fixes worth modeling: a usage ceiling, a metered top tier, or routing heavy outcomes to a cheaper model so your cost per outcome drops.
